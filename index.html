About

Coco Charc Indonesia is the leading supplier of Coconut Charcoal Briquette from Indonesia. We are a company engaged in manufacturing coconut charcoal briquette with high-quality materials.

At 2022, our company was engaged in manufacturing where we processed coconut charcoal briquette. leading manufacturer and exporter of 100% coconut charcoal briquette, from Indonesia. We control every step of the manufacturing and quality control process of our briquette, from coconut shell selection, cleaning and carbonization all the way through to final briquetting, drying and packaging. Our production locations are situated in all the major coconut harvesting regions of Indonesia in Java and Kalimantan. We supply coconut shell charcoal briquette in bulk (up to 200 metric tons per month) and can ship to any destination worldwide. We can produce briquette in any desired shape or size and tailor make packaging according to our customers exact design preferences. We insist on producing our own raw coconut shell charcoal for the majority of our briquette production and also working with a few selected suppliers of high grade charcoal, to ensure that the fixed carbon content is high and ash content low. Our briquette are perfect for use in Shisha/hookah or for barbecue, providing very high heat, long burn time and minimal ash. Due to the high quality charcoal used, our briquette produce no smoke, odors or sparks during burning.

Product
